Privacy

ZIP 4 is built with a privacy-first mindset. This page explains what we do and don’t collect.

No logs. ZIP 4 does not collect your file contents or track what you convert.

What ZIP 4 does

  • Runs conversions locally on your Mac.
  • Uses the minimum data needed to perform the conversion.
  • Does not require an account.

What ZIP 4 does not do

  • No tracking of file names, file contents, or conversion history.
  • No analytics profiling of your behavior.
  • No selling of data — there is no data to sell.
